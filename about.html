About BatModel3D

Our Mission

BatModel3D was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to provide professional-grade 3D models of baseball bats for manufacturers, designers, and sports enthusiasts. Our team combines expertise in 3D modeling with deep knowledge of baseball equipment specifications.

What We Offer

  • High-precision 3D models of baseball bats
  • Custom modeling services for manufacturers
  • Regular updates to our model library
  • Technical support for 3D printing
